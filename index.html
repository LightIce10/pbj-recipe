Peanut Butter and Jelly

Let's learn how to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwhich

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Raspberry Jelly
  • 2 Slices of Bread

Instructions

  1. Lay out 2 slices of bread.
  2. Toast the slices
  3. Spread peanut butter on one slice.
  4. Spread jam on the other slice.
  5. Stick the two slices together and enjoy.
